Password Strength Evaluation - Internship Task 6

Platform: Kali Linux
Tool: passwordmeter.com

Test Results:
1. Weak password: mypassword — Score: 20%
2. Medium password: MyPass123 — Score: 60%
3. Strong password: P@ssW0rd!2025 — Score: 90%
4. Passphrase: Horse-Battery-Staple-2025! — Score: 95%

Observations:
- Longer passwords are significantly harder to crack.
- Mixing character types increases strength.
- Passphrases are strong and memorable.
- Avoid common words and patterns.
